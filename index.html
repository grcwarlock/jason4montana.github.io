Montana House District 37  ·  Independent Candidate  ·  2026

Four generations of Central Montana.

The fourth one is on your ballot.

No party. Just solutions.

Serving at the Defense Intelligence Agency and the Pentagon taught me to read past the summary version of events and find what is actually there. That skill is exactly what Central Montana needs in Helena right now. My great-grandfather was Lewistown's first osteopath. My grandmother was born here in 1920. My grandfather worked as a mining engineer for the Anaconda Company, which is how my mother came into the world in Butte; Central Montana families have always followed the work, then come back. Running as an independent means my obligation runs in one direction: to the residents of District 37, full stop.
